
transform - apply geometric transformations to the input.


This manual page documents briefly the command.

This program follow the usual GNU command line syntax, with long options
starting with two dashes (`-').	 A summary of options is included below.
Rotate around x-axis in degrees).  Rotate around y-axis.  Rotate around z-
axis.  Scale by Scale x-axis by Scale y-axis by Scale z-axis by Translate of
along x-axis.  Translate of along y-axis.  Translate of along z-axis.  Turn
surface inside out.  Fit the resulting surface in a cube of size 1 centered at
the origin.  Print statistics about the surface.  Display the help and exit.
