skill, snice - send a signal or report process status

defined.  Consider using the and commands instead.  The default signal for
skill is TERM.	Use -l or -L to list available signals.	 Particularly useful
signals include HUP, INT, KILL, STOP, CONT, and 0.  Alternate signals may be
specified in three ways: -9 -SIGKILL -KILL.  The default priority for snice is
+4.  Priority numbers range from +20 (slowest) to -20 (fastest).  Negative
priority numbers are restricted to administrative users.  Fast mode.  This
