load_policy - load a new SELinux policy into the kernel

[-qi]
load_policy loads the installed policy file into the kernel.  The existing
policy boolean values are automatically preserved across policy reloads rather
than being reset to the default values in the policy file.

suppress warning messages.  initial policy load. Only use this if this is the
first time policy is being loaded since boot (usually called from initramfs).

enforcing mode requested
