sleep - delay for a specified amount of time NUMBER[SUFFIX]...
OPTION Pause for NUMBER seconds, where NUMBER is an integer or floating-point.
SUFFIX may be 's','m','h', or 'd', for seconds, minutes, hours, days.  With
multiple arguments, pause for the sum of their values.	--help display this
