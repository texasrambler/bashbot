cg_diff - (deprecated) diffs two Cachegrind output files cg_diff [options]
cachegrind-out-file1 cachegrind-out-file2 cg_diff diffs two Cachegrind output
files into a single Cachegrind output file. It is deprecated because
cg_annotate can now do much the same thing, but better.	 -h --help  Show the
help message.  --version  Show the version number.  --mod-filename=<expr>
[default: none] Specifies an s/old/new/ search-and-replace expression that is
applied to all filenames.  --mod-funcname=<expr> [default: none] Like
--mod-filename, but for filenames.  cg_annotate(1), valgrind(1),
$INSTALL/share/doc/valgrind/html/index.html or
http://www.valgrind.org/docs/manual/index.html.	 Nicholas Nethercote.
