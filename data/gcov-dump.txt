gcov-dump - offline gcda and gcno profile dump tool gcov-dump [-v|--version]
     [-h|--help]
     [-l|--long]
     [-p|--positions]
     [-r|--raw]
     [-s|--stable]
     gcovfiles gcov-dump is a tool you can use in conjunction with GCC to dump
content of gcda and gcno profile files offline.	 Display help about using
gcov-dump (on the standard output), and exit without doing any further
processing.  Dump content of records.  Dump positions of records.  Print
content records in raw format.	Print content in stable format usable for
comparison.  Display the gcov-dump version number (on the standard output),
