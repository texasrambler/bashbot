recode-sr-latin - convert Serbian text from Cyrillic to Latin script [OPTION]
Recode Serbian text from Cyrillic to Latin script.  The input text is read
from standard input.  The converted text is output to standard output.	-h,
--help display this help and exit -V, --version output version information and
