diffimg - Calculates intersection between two images diffimg generates an
image where each pixel is the difference between the corresponding pixel in
each of the two source images.	Thus, if the source images are the same the
resulting image will be black, otherwise it will have regions of non-black
where the images differ.  Currently supports: .png, .gif, .jpg, and .ps by
using ghostscript diffimg was written by John Ellson <ellson@research.att.com>
This manual page was written by David Claughton <dave@eclecticdave.com>, for
the Debian project (but may be used by others).
