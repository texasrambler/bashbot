cracklib-check - Check passwords using libcrack2

takes a list of passwords from stdin and checks them via libcrack2's sub
routine.

prints each checked password and the corresponding result of to stdout. The
password and the result are separated by a colon.

GNU/Linux System (but may be used by others).
